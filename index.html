How We Started

North Superior Air Testing and Rental began with a simple realization: too many people were living in homes that were silently making them sick. We saw families struggling with unexplained allergies, fatigue, and respiratory issues, only to eventually discover hidden mold was the culprit.

We also noticed a fundamental flaw in the industry. Often, the companies testing for mold were the exact same companies charging thousands of dollars to fix it. We realized homeowners needed an unbiased, third-party advocate. That's where we come in. We don't do the fixing—our sole mission is to find the problem, provide certified lab results, and ensure the truth about your air quality is brought to light. We are entirely committed to providing you with a safe house space.

Our Services

Initial IAQ Testing

We conduct thorough indoor air quality assessments to identify mold spores, allergens, and other hidden airborne contaminants that could be impacting your health.

EMSL Certified Lab Analysis

We don't guess. All of our air and surface samples are sent directly to EMSL Analytical, a highly trusted and certified laboratory, ensuring you receive accurate, legally defensible results.

Post-Remediation Verification

Did the mold remediation company actually do their job? We offer independent clearance testing and contract verification to double-check their work, ensuring absolutely no mold residue is left behind before you move back into the space.
